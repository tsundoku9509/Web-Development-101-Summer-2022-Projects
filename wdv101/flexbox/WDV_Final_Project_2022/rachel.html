Rachel Willard - CTO/Lead Volunteer Coordinator












  • Rachel's my name, saving pets is my game! I am honored to be the CTO/Lead Volunteer Coordinator for such an amazing organization as Pets To Go.
  • A little about me: I was raised out west, in the mountain air of Colorado. You might be wondering why I would ever think to move out to the farm fields of Illinois, yet the call was too good to pass up!
  • After graduating from college with a double major in Computer Science and Journalism, my heart was set on Pets To Go, which I had interned at as I have family from Springfield.
  • I have loved every second of these last five years and cannot wait to work with you, intrepid volunteer!
